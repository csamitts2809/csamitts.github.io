About Founder

I, Amitt Senha, am the founder of our Firm and recently commenced practice in 2021. I am a qualified Company Secretary and admitted to practice vide registration no. 24969 by The Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI). In addition to this, I am a qualified lawyer with a total experience of more than 15 years.

Before entering dynamic active practice, I had a vast experience having worked with corporates like Stock Exchanges, Shipping, Real Estate, etc. My last stint was as Company Secretary and Legal lead with Gwalior Smart City Development Corporation Limited, a prestigious joint venture with Central and State Governments.

Our ultimate aim is to build long-term relationships with all our clients. We believe that the absolute essence of building such a relationship is the uncompromising quality of service and the targeted expertise that we provide.

In addition to the standard corporate compliance experience, we are rendering specialized services in the areas of Corporate Laws, Corporate Governance issues, drafting/vetting of agreements with merchants, distributors, retailers, Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs), Employment contracts, and Corporate Restructuring. With a vast network of associates spread across various cities in India, we seamlessly deal with Intellectual Property Rights (IPRs), FEMA regulations, Listing compliances, and funding of Corporates, apart from routine incorporation of companies and annual filings with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA).

The strategic combination of deep-dive corporate expertise and local market knowledge enables us to remain highly competitive without ever compromising on quality deliverables.

Corporate & Regulatory Advisory Services

Click on any capability below to review our focus parameters and execution framework:

Company Incorporation & Business Structuring

Every successful business begins with the right legal foundation. We assist entrepreneurs, startups, family businesses, and investors in establishing suitable business structures, including Private Limited Companies, Public Companies, One Person Companies (OPCs), Section 8 Companies, and Limited Liability Partnerships (LLPs). Our services cover end-to-end incorporation support, regulatory registrations, documentation, and post-incorporation compliance guidance, ensuring a smooth and legally compliant business launch.

Compliance Retainership Services

Regulatory compliance is not a one-time activity but an ongoing responsibility. Our retainership services provide businesses with continuous professional support for corporate, secretarial, and regulatory matters. Acting as your extended compliance partner, we assist in maintaining statutory records, conducting periodic compliance reviews, advising management on regulatory developments, and ensuring timely filings under applicable laws.

Legal & Secretarial Due Diligence

In today’s dynamic business environment, informed decisions require thorough verification and risk assessment. We conduct comprehensive legal and secretarial due diligence for mergers, acquisitions, investments, fundraising transactions, and strategic business decisions. Our reviews focus on statutory compliance, corporate records, governance practices, regulatory risks, and potential liabilities, helping stakeholders make confident and informed decisions.

Corporate Governance Advisory

Strong governance frameworks create sustainable businesses and inspire stakeholder confidence. We assist companies in implementing robust corporate governance practices that promote transparency, accountability, ethical decision-making, and regulatory compliance. Our advisory services support boards, promoters, and management teams in establishing governance structures aligned with business objectives and regulatory expectations.

FEMA & Cross-Border Compliance Services

Global business opportunities often involve complex foreign exchange regulations. We provide advisory and compliance support under the Foreign Exchange Management Act (FEMA) for foreign investments, overseas investments, share transfers involving non-residents, reporting requirements, and other cross-border transactions. Our objective is to help businesses navigate international transactions while remaining fully compliant with applicable regulations.

Investor Facilitation & Fundraising Support

Access to capital is critical for business growth. Through our network of professionals and investment associates, we facilitate connections between businesses and potential investors. We assist in preparing investment-ready documentation, conducting preliminary compliance reviews, and coordinating with stakeholders throughout the fundraising process, helping businesses present themselves effectively to prospective investors.

Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) Support

Innovation and brand value are among the most valuable assets of any business. Through our dedicated team of IPR professionals, we assist clients in protecting and managing intellectual property, including trademarks, copyrights, patents, and related rights. Our services are designed to safeguard business identity, innovation, and competitive advantage in an increasingly knowledge-driven economy.

Secretarial Audit & Regulatory Compliance Management

Compliance is a critical component of corporate sustainability. We provide comprehensive secretarial and regulatory compliance support, including compliance audits, corporate restructuring assistance, board and shareholder meeting support, statutory documentation, and advisory services under various corporate laws. Our focus is to minimize regulatory risk and enable businesses to operate with confidence.

Business Advisory & Special Assignments

Every business faces unique challenges requiring practical and legally sound solutions. We provide customized advisory services on corporate restructuring, capital transactions, governance matters, strategic regulatory planning, and other corporate law-related assignments. By combining legal insight with commercial understanding, we help businesses address complex situations efficiently and effectively.
