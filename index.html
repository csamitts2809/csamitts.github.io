About Founder

I, Amitt Senha, am the founder of our Firm and recently commenced practice in 2021. I am a qualified Company Secretary and admitted to practice vide registration no. 24969 by The Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI). In addition to this, I am a qualified lawyer with a total experience of more than 15 years.

Before entering dynamic active practice, I had a vast experience having worked with corporates like Stock Exchanges, Shipping, Real Estate, etc. My last stint was as Company Secretary and Legal lead with Gwalior Smart City Development Corporation Limited, a prestigious joint venture with Central and State Governments.

Our ultimate aim is to build long-term relationships with all our clients. We believe that the absolute essence of building such a relationship is the uncompromising quality of service and the targeted expertise that we provide.

In addition to the standard corporate compliance experience, we are rendering specialized services in the areas of Corporate Laws, Corporate Governance issues, drafting/vetting of agreements with merchants, distributors, retailers, Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs), Employment contracts, and Corporate Restructuring. With a vast network of associates spread across various cities in India, we seamlessly deal with Intellectual Property Rights (IPRs), FEMA regulations, Listing compliances, and funding of Corporates, apart from routine incorporation of companies and annual filings with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA).

The strategic combination of deep-dive corporate expertise and local market knowledge enables us to remain highly competitive without ever compromising on quality deliverables.
